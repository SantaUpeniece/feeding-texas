As the Client Voices Coordinator, Jen works to gather and promote voices of Texans from across the state who experience hunger. Jen manages a qualitative database of stories of hunger and collaborates with food banks throughout the Feeding Texas network to set standards for ethical story collection and community engagement. Prior to becoming the Client Voices Coordinator, Jen spent a year at Feeding Texas through the AmeriCorps VISTA program.
